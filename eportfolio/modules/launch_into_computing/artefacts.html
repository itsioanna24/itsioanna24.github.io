Artefacts

This page collates the artefacts produced across the twelve units of the Launch into Computing module. It includes contributions from three collaborative discussions, three individual e-portfolio activities, a team design proposal for a multi-agent academic research system, and an individual implementation and presentation. Together, these artefacts document my engagement with the module's core themes: intelligent agent architectures, multi-agent coordination, agent communication languages, natural language processing, deep learning, and the ethical dimensions of autonomous systems.
