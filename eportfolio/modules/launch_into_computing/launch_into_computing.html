Launch into Computing

This module served as my introduction to postgraduate-level computing studies with intensive exploration across multiple computing disciplines. Through this foundational course, I gained exposure to Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, Data Science, and Enterprise IT Management, developing both theoretical understanding and hands-on experience with industry-relevant challenges. The module emphasised the development of critical analysis skills and professional competencies, while fostering an appreciation for the ethical dimensions of computing practice. A key focus throughout was understanding how computing technologies can drive positive change in areas of equality, diversity, and inclusion within modern business environments.

Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of this module, I:

  • Analysed and addressed real-world computing challenges in modern business systems.
  • Researched and synthesised information from diverse sources to support design and problem-solving.
  • Applied suitable tools, methodologies, and techniques to tackle computing issues and assess their impact.
  • Evaluated legal, ethical, social, and professional responsibilities in computing practice.
  • Reflected on my personal growth while exploring the role of computing in promoting equality, diversity, and inclusion.
