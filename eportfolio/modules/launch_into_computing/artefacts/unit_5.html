Reflection on this Artefact

This post demonstrates a clear understanding of how data science and storage technologies support large-scale platforms like Netflix. A key strength is the effective link between machine learning and business impact, particularly the role of recommendation systems in driving user engagement. The use of real-world technologies such as Amazon Web Services and Apache Cassandra strengthens the practical relevance of the discussion.
However, the post is largely descriptive and could benefit from deeper critical evaluation. While NoSQL databases are presented as ideal, important trade-offs—such as eventual consistency and increased system complexity—are not considered. Including comparisons with SQL-based approaches would demonstrate stronger analytical depth.
The discussion of data privacy is a valuable inclusion, highlighting ethical concerns in behavioural analytics. The example of regulatory action adds credibility, but this section could be improved by linking ethical challenges to technical solutions, such as data anonymisation.
Overall, this reflection helped me recognise the importance of balancing technical performance with ethical responsibility. In future work, I will aim to incorporate more critical analysis and evaluation of alternative approaches.
