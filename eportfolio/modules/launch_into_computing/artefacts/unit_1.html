Case Study: AI-Powered Assistants in Business Transformation

Discussion Forum

Reflection on this Artefact

This artefact reflects a deeper understanding of AI as both a technological and strategic driver of business transformation. Initially, my focus was on the operational benefits of AI, such as automation, efficiency, and improved decision-making. However, through engaging with examples like Walmart’s Intelligent Retail Lab and supporting literature, I recognised how AI can reshape entire business processes through predictive analytics and real-time data use.
More importantly, this discussion increased my awareness of the ethical challenges linked to AI adoption. Issues such as data privacy, algorithmic bias, and lack of transparency highlight that technical performance alone is not enough. I found it particularly significant that many organisations still lack formal ethical frameworks, which can lead to risks in accountability and fairness. This shifted my perspective to view ethics as a core component of AI development rather than an afterthought.
Additionally, exploring contributions from cybersecurity and software engineering reinforced the interdisciplinary nature of AI. It became clear that effective AI systems rely on secure infrastructures and strong engineering foundations. Overall, this reflection demonstrates my progression towards a more critical and balanced understanding of AI in business contexts.

Discussion Forum

Reflection on this Artefact

Writing this discussion post allowed me to deepen my understanding of how data science is applied within the financial sector, particularly through FinTech innovations. By exploring real-world examples such as Coinbase and Capital One, I was able to connect theoretical concepts like machine learning and predictive analytics to practical applications, which strengthened my ability to critically evaluate industry practices.
One key insight I gained is how central data science has become in enabling real-time decision-making, especially in areas such as fraud detection and risk management. At the same time, researching this topic highlighted the ethical challenges associated with these technologies, particularly algorithmic bias and data privacy. This made me more aware that technical innovation must be balanced with responsible and ethical considerations.
Reflecting on my work, I believe the post effectively combines academic references with practical examples. However, I could improve by providing more critical analysis rather than mainly descriptive content, and by exploring opposing perspectives in greater depth.
Overall, this task helped me develop my research, academic writing, and critical thinking skills, which are essential for my progression in the MSc programme.
