Induction Module

This module provided an introduction to the University of Essex Online learning environment, key academic skills, and the resources available to support online study. Over the course of the induction, I became familiar with Moodle, the library, the Study Skills Hub, and essential strategies for reflective learning and critical thinking.

Learning Outcomes

During this module, I developed the technical and personal competencies needed for online learning, including effective use of the virtual learning environment, academic writing, research skills, and correct referencing. I also gained an understanding of how to avoid academic dishonesty, participate in learning communities, and organise my studies effectively.
