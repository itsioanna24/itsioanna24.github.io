Discussion Forum

The Importance of E-Portfolios in Postgraduate Education

For my first assignment, I contributed to a discussion forum on the role of e-portfolios in postgraduate education. I explored how e-portfolios serve not only as a collection of academic work but also as a powerful tool for reflection, professional growth, and career advancement. Drawing on recent research, I emphasised their increasing use since the COVID-19 pandemic, their role in helping students set learning goals, and their ability to differentiate graduates in a competitive IT job market.

Tutor Feedback: "Good work. A good variety of distinct opinions are presented, and related academic literature was well used. Remember to define acronyms on first use and follow UK English conventions."

Reflection: This task helped me understand the importance of clear academic writing conventions, such as defining acronyms (e.g., Artificial Intelligence (AI)) and adhering to UK English standards. It reinforced how e-portfolios can be used to showcase growth, potential, and professional skills in a meaningful way.

Induction Assignment

The importance of a MSc in Artificial Intelligence

This artefact is an essay exploring the importance of a postgraduate degree in Artificial Intelligence. It discusses the history and principles of AI, its real-world applications across healthcare, finance, and transportation, and the growing demand for AI skills. The essay also reflects on the MSc programme, highlighting modules like Machine Learning and Numerical Analysis, the development of research and technical skills, and the ethical considerations surrounding AI. Additionally, it outlines personal motivations for pursuing AI studies, including career transition from web development to AI engineering and the enhancement of an ePortfolio.

Tutor Feedback:
  • Excellent opening and overall written expression.
  • Minor grammatical errors; future works should refine text further.
  • Consider using section headers to better organize content for the reader.
  • Addresses the set question and provides good understanding of the field.
  • Opportunity to explore a specific case study more in-depth.
  • Appropriate use of statistics to contextualize discussion.
  • Good attention to academic integrity: acronyms defined, references well used and listed alphabetically.
  • Ensure a wider variety of sources to avoid over-reliance on a single author.
  • Word count noted as 792/750, which meets requirements.
