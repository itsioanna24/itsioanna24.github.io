Understanding Artificial Intelligence

This module introduced me to the foundations and applications of Artificial Intelligence, exploring its history, principles, and ethical implications alongside its growing impact in industry and society. I examined fundamental concepts such as the Turing Test, knowledge representation, machine learning algorithms and data preparation, while also engaging in practical programming exercises to implement learning systems. The module highlighted the professional and ethical responsibilities of AI practitioners, encouraging reflection on the societal challenges and opportunities presented by AI.
