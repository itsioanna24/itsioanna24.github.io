Reflection on this Artefact

This initial post framed machine learning within the evolution from Industry 4.0 to Industry 5.0, using the 2022 Toyota cyberattack to argue that the incident exposed the sociotechnical assumptions of optimisation-driven ML deployment rather than a failure of the technology itself. Preparing this post shifted how I thought about model evaluation — moving beyond accuracy toward resilience, governance, and human oversight.
