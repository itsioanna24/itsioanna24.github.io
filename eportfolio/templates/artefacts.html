Here is a detailed showcase of my work during the Induction Module, including assignment summaries, discussion contributions, and tutor feedback.

Discussion Forum

Discussion Forum Contribution: The importance of e-portfolios in Postgraduate Education

In this activity, I explored my preferred learning style using the VARK questionnaire. I identified as a multimodal learner, benefiting from visual aids, hands-on practice, and discussion. In the forum, I shared my results, explained how they shaped my approach to online study, and engaged in conversation with peers to compare learning preferences.

Through peer interaction, I developed a deeper appreciation for diverse learning strategies and gained insights into how others adapt to challenges in virtual learning environments.

Tutor Feedback: "Your reflections on multimodal learning were well articulated and demonstrated self-awareness. The engagement with peers showed good academic collaboration."

Reflection: This task helped me understand the importance of clear academic writing conventions, such as defining acronyms (e.g., Artificial Intelligence (AI)) and adhering to UK English standards. It reinforced how e-portfolios can be used to showcase growth, potential, and professional skills in a meaningful way.
